The Tang lab focuses on understanding the mechanisms behind infectious diseases and developing innovative therapies.

Our primary research interests revolve around utilizing Listeria monocytogenes and Staphylococcus aureus as model strains to investigate antimicrobial resistance, bacterial pathogenesis, and the interactions between hosts and microbes.

The Tang lab is actively recruiting enthusiastic graduate students and postdocs to join the group. Please checkout our research to learn about current research directions.
